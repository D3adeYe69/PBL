Setting Up Your Profile
Adding a Profile Photo
  1. Tap User Information.
  2. Tap Edit data.
  3. Tap Change photo.
  4. Select Upload photo from your device.
  5. Crop your selected photo then tap Save.
Changing Your Email
  1. Tap User Information.
  2. Tap Edit data.
  3. Tap Change email.
  4. Enter new email.
  5. Tap Save.
