Troubleshooting

If you are experiencing problems with our app, try working through these steps:

Latest version

Make sure your device is running the latest version of the HabitQuest app.

Restart the app and your device

As a first step, we recommend restarting the app and device. This can often resolve issues or free up storage resources.

Check your internet connection

Weak data or Wi-Fi signal can also impact your experience.

Clear app storage

Try clearing your app cache. This removes temporary files that may be causing issues.
